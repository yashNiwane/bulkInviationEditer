Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How does this batch image editor work?

Our tool uses Fabric.js to create a dynamic canvas. When you upload an image, it becomes the background. The text you add is an editable object on top. When you generate, the tool loops through your list of data, replacing the text for each item and exporting a new high-resolution image for each one before zipping them for download.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All image processing and data handling happens directly in your browser. Your images and data from your Excel file are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

What are the best use cases for this tool?

This tool is perfect for anyone needing to create multiple, similar images with slight variations. Common uses include: generating personalized certificates of completion, creating custom invitations with guest names, producing social media graphics with unique codes, or making personalized marketing materials.
